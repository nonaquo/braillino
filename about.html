About Braillino

Bridging the gap between digital text and tactile Braille

Our Story

Braillino was created with a simple but powerful goal: to make text accessible through touch. We believe that everyone should be able to experience written words in a way that works for them.

By combining Arduino hardware with an intuitive web interface, we've made it possible for anyone to convert digital text into physical Braille patterns instantly. What started as a project has grown into a tool that brings words to life through technology.

✿ Accessibility

Making information available to everyone, regardless of how they experience the world

✿ Simplicity

Complex technology should feel effortless and intuitive to use

✿ Innovation

Combining hardware and software to create new ways of interacting with text

Our Mission

To create technology that breaks down barriers and opens up new ways of experiencing information. We're committed to making the digital world more accessible, one word at a time.
